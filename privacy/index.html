Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

1. Who we are

Jeema is a competitive running app. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at naidoo.mishay@gmail.com.

2. What we collect and why

Data Why
Email address To create and authenticate your account
Precise GPS location To record your run route and calculate pace in real time
Run stats (distance, pace, elevation, duration, route) To display your history, power leaderboards, and ranked matchmaking
Profile photo To display your avatar to other users
Run captions and comments To power the activity feed
Username and user ID To identify you across ranked matches, leaderboards, and the social feed

3. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to operate the app — run tracking, ranked matchmaking, leaderboards, and the activity feed. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-party services

We use Supabase to store account and run data securely in the cloud. You can read their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

5. Location data

Location is only collected while a run is active and the app is open or running in the background. We do not collect your location at any other time.

6. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing naidoo.mishay@gmail.com.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the email above.

8. Children

Jeema is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
